COURTYARD COLLECTIVE

How to Send Submissions

Submission Email: courtyardsubmissions@gmail.com

Written Submissions:Send email to courtyardsubmission@gmail.com briefly describing what your wwritten piece is about. Attach piece either as a Word document or shared Google document. Include your name and pronouns.

Photography Submissions:Send photos or video through email or share through Google Drive. You many include captions you would like to attach to each photo, or you may write a brief synopsis describing you work(not required). Include your name and pronouns.

Art Submissions: Send a scan of your work or photos of your work through email. Include title of piece, medium, and year you created it. Also include name and pronouns.
